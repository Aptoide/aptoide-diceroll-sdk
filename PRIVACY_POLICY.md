# Privacy Policy for Aptoide Diceroll SDK
**Last Updated: March 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and process data through our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number. However, we use third-party tools that collect technical identifiers to help our application function correctly and to measure our performance.

## 2. Third-Party Service Providers
We use **AppsFlyer** as our primary attribution and analytics provider. This service helps us understand how users find our app and ensures that in-app features (like purchases) are delivered successfully.

AppsFlyer may collect and process the following data:
* **Device Identifiers:** Such as your IP address, IDFA (Apple), or GAID (Google Advertising ID).
* **Event Data:** Technical logs of specific actions within the app, such as "App Installation" or "Purchase Completed."

**Purpose:** This data is used solely to measure marketing performance, prevent fraud, and provide customer support for transaction verifications.

## 3. Your Rights (GDPR / DMA)
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have specific rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Digital Markets Act (DMA):
* **Access & Deletion:** You have the right to request access to or deletion of the data processed.
* **Withdrawal of Consent:** You can opt-out of data collection at any time via the **Settings** menu within the application.

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties. Data is shared only with:
* **AppsFlyer:** Our data processor.
* **Integrated Partners:** For the strictly limited purpose of install attribution and measuring ad effectiveness.

## 5. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the information processed via AppsFlyer from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
